Frequently Asked Questions

How do I schedule a ride?

Go to the “Start a Ride” page, choose your time and destination, then click “Schedule”.

Can I cancel my ride?

Yes, open your scheduled rides and click “Cancel Ride”.

How do I contact my driver?

You can message the driver from the ride details page.
